1.1 Characteristic of Living Organism

Movement

Move parts of themselves to a new area


Respiration

Chemical reaction in cell that break down nutrient and relese energy


Sensitivity

Ability to detect or sense stimuli and make an apropriate responses


Growth

Increase in size and dry mass

Removal of the waste product of metabolism

Obtaining food
